Series · 2022 – ongoing

Common Grounds

An observational series made in shared public spaces: the streets, sidewalks, markets, bus stops. These photographs observe the unremarkable moments of urban coexistence and how people claim space, navigate proximity, and move through the same world without entering each other's.
